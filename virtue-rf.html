The Technology

What Is the Cartessa™ Virtue RF?

The Cartessa™ Virtue RF is a state-of-the-art radiofrequency microneedling system that combines two proven anti-aging technologies in a single treatment. Ultra-fine, insulated needles create precise micro-channels in the skin while simultaneously delivering controlled RF energy deep into the dermis — the layer where collagen and elastin are produced.


Unlike traditional microneedling, the Virtue RF's intelligent delivery system protects the epidermis while maximizing thermal energy in the dermis, producing dramatic skin-remodeling results with far less discomfort and downtime than ablative procedures. The device features both 36-pin and coagulation tips, allowing our providers to customize every treatment to your skin type and concern.

The Process

How the Virtue RF Works

Each session follows a careful, patient-first process to ensure your comfort, safety, and optimal outcomes.

01

Consultation & Assessment

Your provider evaluates your skin concerns, medical history, and goals. A customized treatment plan — including depth settings and tip selection — is designed specifically for you.

02

Topical Numbing

A prescription-strength numbing cream is applied to the treatment area approximately 30–45 minutes prior to the procedure to maximize your comfort throughout.

03

RF Microneedling

The Virtue RF handpiece glides across your skin, delivering precise RF energy through insulated gold-plated microneedles at controlled depths — triggering your body's natural healing and collagen-building response.

04

Soothing & Recovery

A calming serum and SPF are applied immediately after. Mild redness and warmth are normal and typically resolve within 24–48 hours, leaving you with a refreshed, glowing complexion.

Is It Right For You?

Ideal Candidates & Considerations

Great Candidates Include Those With:

  • Fine lines, wrinkles, or skin laxity
  • Enlarged pores or uneven skin texture
  • Mild to moderate skin sagging on the face or neck
  • Atrophic acne scars or surgical scars
  • Sun damage or rough, dull skin tone
  • Stretch marks on the body
  • Any skin tone — Fitzpatrick types I–VI
  • Adults seeking non-surgical skin rejuvenation

Treatment May Not Be Ideal If You:

  • Are pregnant or nursing
  • Have active acne, eczema, or rosacea flare in the treatment zone
  • Have a pacemaker or implanted metal device near the area
  • Have taken isotretinoin (Accutane) within the last 6 months
  • Have a history of keloid scarring
  • Have an active skin infection or open wound in the area

A thorough consultation with our medical team will determine if Virtue RF is the right treatment for your specific needs and health history.

Have Questions?

Virtue RF FAQs

How many Virtue RF treatments will I need?

Most patients achieve optimal results with a series of 3–4 treatments spaced 4–6 weeks apart. However, some patients see significant improvement after just one session. Your provider will recommend the ideal number based on your individual skin concerns and goals.

Does the Virtue RF treatment hurt?

Topical numbing cream is applied 30–45 minutes before treatment to minimize discomfort. Most patients describe the sensation as a warm, prickly feeling. The Virtue RF is widely regarded as one of the more comfortable RF microneedling platforms available.

How soon will I see results?

Many patients notice an immediate glow and subtle firming effect. More significant collagen remodeling results — improved texture, tightness, and scar reduction — typically become visible 4–8 weeks after treatment and continue improving for up to 6 months.

What is the downtime after Virtue RF?

Most patients experience redness, mild swelling, and a sunburn-like sensation for 24–48 hours. Some may experience slight pinpoint bruising. Makeup can typically be applied 24 hours post-treatment and most daily activities resume within 1–2 days.

Can Virtue RF be combined with other treatments?

Yes — Virtue RF pairs beautifully with BOTOX®, dermal fillers, chemical peels, and the Cartessa PHYSIQ body contouring system. Your provider will build a customized combination plan to maximize your results, spacing treatments appropriately to allow for healing.

Is Virtue RF safe for darker skin tones?

Absolutely. Unlike many laser-based treatments, Virtue RF's insulated needle technology delivers energy directly to the dermis while protecting the epidermis — making it one of the safest resurfacing options for patients with medium to deep skin tones (Fitzpatrick IV–VI) with low risk of post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation.

How is Virtue RF different from regular microneedling?

Traditional microneedling creates surface-level micro-channels to stimulate collagen. Virtue RF delivers radiofrequency energy deep into the dermis through each needle, generating significantly more heat-induced collagen remodeling — resulting in more pronounced skin tightening and textural improvement than standard microneedling alone.
