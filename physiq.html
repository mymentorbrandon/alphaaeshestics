The Technology

What Is the Cartessa™ PHYSIQ?

The Cartessa™ PHYSIQ is a next-generation, non-invasive body contouring system that addresses two of the most common body goals simultaneously: fat reduction and muscle toning. Powered by exclusive STEP Technology (Sequential Thermal & Electrical Pulse), PHYSIQ is the only device on the market that delivers deep-tissue laser energy and electrical muscle stimulation — either separately or in one combined session.


PHYSIQ's laser energy (SDM — Superluminescent Diode Matrix) heats fat cells to the point of natural cell death, while EMS electrodes trigger powerful muscle contractions that exercise and strengthen the underlying tissue. With four independent applicators, multiple body zones can be treated at the same time, making it one of the most efficient body treatments available.

The Process

How PHYSIQ Works

Each PHYSIQ session is comfortable, hands-free, and designed to maximize your results in the shortest possible time.

01

Consultation & Goal Setting

Your provider assesses your body concerns and goals. A personalized treatment plan is created — including which areas to target, which applicator combination to use, and how many sessions are recommended.

02

Applicator Placement

Up to four applicators are positioned on the target areas. Because of their compact, flexible design, applicators conform to curves and can address multiple body zones — including smaller areas — simultaneously.

03

STEP Technology Delivery

Laser energy (SDM) heats and destroys fat cells while EMS electrodes trigger deep muscle contractions — all in the same session. The tissue quickly reaches therapeutic temperature while surface cooling keeps the skin comfortable throughout.

04

Immediate Return to Life

There is no downtime with PHYSIQ. You can resume all normal activities immediately after each session. Your provider may recommend post-treatment massage with PHYSIQ body lotion to enhance results over the following weeks.

Is It Right For You?

Ideal Candidates & Considerations

Great Candidates Include Those Who:

  • Are near their ideal body weight but have stubborn fat pockets
  • Want to tone and strengthen muscle without intense exercise
  • Are experiencing muscle loss or skin laxity from rapid weight loss
  • Are on a medical weight loss program and want to complement results
  • Prefer a non-surgical, zero-downtime body treatment
  • Want to treat multiple body areas in a single session
  • Are looking for a long-term solution with lasting fat cell elimination

Treatment May Not Be Ideal If You:

  • Are pregnant or nursing
  • Have a pacemaker or implanted metal device near the treatment area
  • Have active skin infections or open wounds in the area
  • Are seeking a primary weight loss solution (PHYSIQ is a body contouring, not weight loss, treatment)
  • Have severe obesity — results are best for those near their goal weight
  • Have certain nerve or muscle disorders

A thorough consultation with our medical team will determine if PHYSIQ is the right fit for your body goals and health history.

Have Questions?

PHYSIQ FAQs

How many PHYSIQ treatments will I need?

Most patients achieve optimal results with a series of 5 treatments, scheduled once per week. Some patients notice visible improvements after just their first session, with results continuing to improve over the following weeks and months as the body naturally processes eliminated fat cells and builds new muscle tissue.

Does PHYSIQ hurt?

PHYSIQ is well tolerated by most patients. The laser energy produces a gentle warming sensation, while EMS creates muscle contractions similar to an intense workout. Your provider can adjust intensity settings to keep you comfortable throughout. There is no pain, no numbing cream required, and no recovery discomfort afterward.

When will I see results?

Many patients notice initial improvements in tone and firmness after the first few sessions. More significant fat reduction and muscle definition typically become visible 4–8 weeks after completing your treatment series, as the body continues to process and eliminate fat cells and muscle tissue strengthens progressively.

Is there any downtime after PHYSIQ?

No — PHYSIQ requires zero downtime. You can return to all normal activities immediately following each session. Some patients experience mild warmth or muscle soreness similar to post-workout fatigue, which resolves quickly. Your provider may recommend a post-treatment massage with PHYSIQ body lotion to optimize results.

How is PHYSIQ different from other body contouring devices?

PHYSIQ is the only body contouring system that combines both fat-reducing laser energy (SDM) and muscle-stimulating EMS via exclusive STEP Technology in a single treatment. It also features four independent applicators that can be adjusted and programmed separately, allowing multiple body areas to be treated simultaneously — something most competitors cannot offer.

Can PHYSIQ be combined with other treatments?

Absolutely. PHYSIQ pairs especially well with our medical weight loss programs and with the Cartessa™ Virtue RF for patients addressing both body contouring and skin laxity. Your provider will build a comprehensive plan, spacing treatments appropriately for the best overall outcome.

Are PHYSIQ results permanent?

The fat cells destroyed by PHYSIQ's laser energy are eliminated permanently — they do not regenerate. However, maintaining results long-term requires a healthy lifestyle with regular exercise and a balanced diet. Muscle tone results are progressive and are best maintained with consistent physical activity following your treatment series.
