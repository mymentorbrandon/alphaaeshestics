OUR SKIN SERVICES

What We Offer

VI PEEL

A medical-grade chemical peel that addresses sun damage, hyperpigmentation, fine lines, acne scars, and uneven texture. Results in smoother, brighter, younger-looking skin in as little as 7 days. Multiple formulations available to target your specific concerns.

SIGNATURE ALPHA FACIAL

Our 45-minute signature facial is a deeply personalized experience — cleansing, exfoliation, extractions, and nourishing masks — designed to leave your skin glowing. Perfect for maintenance and first-time clients.

SPORTS FACIAL

A 60-minute advanced facial targeting the specific skin challenges of active individuals — sweat-related breakouts, enlarged pores, and dehydration. Great for men and women with active lifestyles.

TEEN FACIAL

A gentle 30-minute facial formulated for younger skin. Targets acne, clogged pores, and oiliness in a calming environment that teaches teens good skincare habits early.

RED LIGHT THERAPY

NASA-developed technology now used in top clinics worldwide. Red light stimulates collagen production, reduces inflammation, accelerates healing, and boosts cellular energy. No downtime, no discomfort. Great as a standalone or add-on.
