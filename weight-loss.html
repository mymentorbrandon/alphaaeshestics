OUR MEDICATIONS

GLP-1 Weight Loss Protocols

SEMAGLUTIDE

Semaglutide Program

Semaglutide (the active ingredient in Ozempic® and Wegovy®) is a GLP-1 receptor agonist that mimics a natural hormone to reduce appetite, slow stomach emptying, and regulate blood sugar.

Clinical trials show an average of 15% body weight reduction over 68 weeks. Our protocol starts at a low dose and titrates up based on your response and tolerance.

TIRZEPATIDE

Tirzepatide Program

Tirzepatide (the active ingredient in Mounjaro® and Zepbound®) is a dual GLP-1 and GIP receptor agonist — meaning it targets two hunger hormones simultaneously for even greater weight loss results.

Studies show up to 22% body weight reduction. Available in multiple doses from 2.5mg up to 15mg, titrated under physician supervision.
