OUR STORY & MISSION

Built on Trust. Rooted in Real Health.

Alpha Aesthetics & Health was born from a deeply personal journey. Our founder — a lifelong health advocate who dedicated himself to natural wellness — learned firsthand the real dangers of off-brand peptides and unregulated products flooding the market. After suffering injury from products that simply couldn't be trusted, he made a decision: build a place the Alpharetta community could count on completely.

Every product we use is sourced exclusively from trusted, American-based suppliers. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just clean, proven treatments administered by qualified professionals who genuinely care about your outcome.

That commitment to integrity is why we proudly partner with Always In Good Health — a trusted 20+-year family practice specializing in regenerative medicine, joint pain relief, and chronic pain management. Together, we offer our patients a full continuum of care they can rely on — from advanced aesthetics to deep regenerative healing.

TREATMENTS
What services do you offer?

We offer medical weight loss (including peptide therapy), skin treatments (facials, microneedling, chemical peels), and injectables (BOTOX®, JUVÉDERM®, and more).

Is there a consultation before treatment?

Yes, every new patient receives a thorough consultation to assess your goals and create a customized treatment plan.

Do you offer treatments for men?

Absolutely! Our services, including hormone therapy, weight loss, and skin care, are designed for both women and men.

Will I need downtime after treatment?

Most of our services are minimally invasive with little to no downtime. We'll guide you through what to expect for each treatment.

INJECTABLES
What's the difference between BOTOX® and fillers?

BOTOX® relaxes muscles to reduce wrinkles, while fillers add volume to smooth and sculpt the face.

Yes, we only use FDA-approved brands like BOTOX®, JUVÉDERM®, RADIESSE®, and more.
